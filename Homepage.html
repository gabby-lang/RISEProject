About Ragon

The Ragon Institute, established in 2009, focuses on using the body's immune system to prevent and cure diseases. It researches HIV/AIDS, Global Infectious Diseases, Emerging Infectious Diseases, Vaccine Development, Basic and Applied Immunology, and Clinical Studies. With experts from top institutions, it aims to find solutions for better health, including combating the ongoing pandemic and eradicating HIV through collaboration with South African communities.
